# 005

You calmly walk over to the flying pallas admiring its fluffy wide face. Its brilliant coat almost sparkles in the remaining dusk sunlight as you reach out to beckon its attention. It lets a low corse purr before spreading its fur covered conjoined skin between its legs and torso and glides down to a walkway multiple levels below your current position. Taking a moment, you marvel at the beauty and the likeness of this automaton, and appreciate existing in a time and place where creation is one man's enterprises.
